Creatives and Makers - Dinner Party

April 8, 2025 | 7:00 PM

Join us for an exclusive dinner gathering for like-minded creatives from various industries.

This event is designed for content creators, filmmakers, software engineers, game developers, musicians, and all artists who want to expand their creative circle, find potential collaborators, or simply enjoy an evening with others who understand the creative journey, while also enjoying authentic Italian cuisine.

✨ Three-Course Menu ✨

🍽️ Shared Appetizers

  • Pittule (Traditional Salentinian fried dough puffs)
  • Focaccia with Capocollo and Cheese (Italian cured pork and artisanal cheese focaccia)
  • Grilled Zucchini with herbs and olive oil

🍝 Main Course (choose one)

  • Mafalde with Zucchini and Caciocavallo (Ribbon pasta with zucchini and aged cheese)
  • Tria with Beans and Mussels (Traditional Pugliese pasta with cannellini beans and fresh mussels)
  • Orecchiette with Nduja and Sausage (Ear-shaped pasta with spicy spreadable sausage and Italian pork sausage)

🍮 Dessert

  • Cannolo (Sicilian pastry filled with sweet ricotta cream)

* Vegetarian, Vegan, and Gluten Free options available
